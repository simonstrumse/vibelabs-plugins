# Artifact: Day 1 / 30 / 100 plan

The canonical deal-comms readiness artifact. Brunswick / FGS / Joele Frank / Sard Verbinnen / Kekst CNC convention. Covers the pre-announcement pack + per-horizon stakeholder-engagement cadence.

## Structure

```markdown
# Day 1 / 30 / 100 plan — <deal title>

**Run:** <run-id> · **Deal type:** <acquisition / JV / divestiture / restructuring> · **Close target:** <ISO date or horizon>

## Pre-announcement pack (T-0 ready)

These artifacts must be fully drafted and legal-reviewed before announcement. Grounded in the reactions from this run's cast.

- **Press release** — <status: drafted / reviewed / finalized>. Grounded in the message-house consensus. Specific anticipated reactions it addresses: <list from report's Consensus signals>.
- **Investor presentation** — <status>. Slides covering: strategic rationale (3-5), financial impact (2-3), integration plan (2-3), risk factors (2), Q&A anticipated (5). Grounded in sell-side persona concerns.
- **CEO talking points** — <status>. First-person script for: analyst call opening (2 min), employee town hall opening (3 min), trade-press interview framing (90s). Grounded in message-house pillars.
- **Employee script** — <status>. Manager toolkit + all-hands talking points + named FAQ responses. Grounded in employee persona reactions.
- **Customer letter** — <status>. Tiered: top-10 customer direct call script + mid-tier written note + long-tail email. Grounded in customer persona continuity-risk reactions.
- **Regulator filing** — <status>. Antitrust / financial-regulator notification with pre-filed narrative. Grounded in regulator persona's likely concerns.
- **Analyst call script** — <status>. Full script with Q&A hot-sheet. Grounded in sell-side + buy-side debate phase.

## Day 1 — announcement day (T-0 through T+24h)

**Objective:** Land the narrative. No surprises. Every audience hears the same story in the right channel at the right time.

### Stakeholder sequencing
- **T-12h to T-1h:** Regulator pre-filing (where required).
- **T-1h:** Top-5 institutional holders call (CEO + IR).
- **T-30m:** Embargoed press release to tier-1 financial press.
- **T-0:** Press release public. Regulatory filing public.
- **T+15m:** All-employee email.
- **T+30m:** Analyst call begins.
- **T+1h:** CEO / CFO on tier-1 financial press interviews.
- **T+2h:** Top-10 customer direct calls.
- **T+4h:** All-hands town hall.
- **T+6h:** Customer follow-up note (mid-tier).
- **T+12h:** Post-market analyst check-ins.
- **T+24h:** Day-1 wrap call with comms + IR leadership.

### Risks to watch
- <red-line warning from report>
- <red-line warning from report>
- <leak-risk signal from report>

## Day 30 — integration-signal window

**Objective:** Demonstrate integration competence. Silent signals beat loud ones.

### Milestones
- Integration team staffed and communicated to key stakeholders (completed Day-14).
- First customer-renewal cycle observed: retention signal within 10% of pre-announcement baseline.
- Employee attrition signal: voluntary departures ≤ 110% of trailing-quarter baseline.
- Proxy-advisor engagement (if material).

### Communications
- Day-30 investor note (short, specific integration signals).
- Day-30 internal pulse survey + manager roundtable.
- Customer-advisory call cycle (top-10).

### Risks to watch
- <grounded in Day-30 projection reactions from memory-update phase>

## Day 100 — operating-tempo read

**Objective:** The "did the thesis land" window. Quiet confidence, not declarative victory.

### Milestones
- Integration milestone cadence visible in public signals (job postings, product announcements, customer referenceability).
- First full quarter of combined financials ready for analyst-day or earnings call.
- Employee-voice signal: pulse survey shows stabilization.
- Regulator close-of-review confirmation (where applicable).

### Communications
- Day-100 investor day OR dedicated analyst update.
- Internal "what we've learned" + refreshed strategy communication.
- Customer-level case studies (top-5 by voluntary endorsement).

### Risks to watch
- <grounded in Day-100 projection reactions>

## What this plan does NOT cover

- Post-close reorganization beyond Day 100
- Regulatory remediation (if required) — separate workstream
- Product-roadmap communications — owned by product marketing

## Length target

~1000-1500 words.
```

## Grounding rules

- Every "grounded in" citation traces to a specific section of `report.md` or a specific persona file.
- Day-30 and Day-100 sections require the `memory-update` phase to have run — if absent, those sections are sparse and labeled "projection from Day-0 only."
- Stakeholder sequencing reflects urgency scores from the cast-spec.
- Risk-to-watch items draw from the report's red-line warnings.
