## Grounding rules

- Every dimension reading grounded in at least one verbatim persona quote from findings.
- Workplace dimension always pulled from employee-cluster reactions (the single highest-leverage signal for positioning credibility).
- Citizenship dimension must include an activist-cluster reading if one is in the cast (greenwashing-risk flag).
- Composite read preserves disagreement — if dimensions split between lift and erode, do not collapse to net-neutral.
- Caveats section is mandatory — synthetic reading is directional, not quantitative.
