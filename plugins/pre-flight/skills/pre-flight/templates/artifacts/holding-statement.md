# Artifact: Holding statement

A fill-in-the-blank statement deployable in 15 minutes by the on-duty comms lead. Grounded in the reactions on hand — particularly the red-line warnings and the consensus signals. Designed to buy 2-4 hours before a fuller statement is needed.

## Structure

```markdown
# Holding statement — <stimulus title>

**For immediate release** · **Issued by:** <spokesperson role>
**Approved by:** <approver role> · **Do not use after:** <ISO date + 4 hours>

<!-- 3-5 sentences maximum. Honors every red-line warning surfaced in the report. -->

We are aware of <specific trigger event or publication>. <One sentence acknowledging the legitimate concern without accepting contested claims.>

<One sentence naming the concrete action the organization is taking now — not a promise, a step already in motion.>

<One sentence on when / how more information will follow.>

For further comment, contact <media contact>.

---

**Do not say:** <list of phrases the holding statement must not contain — pulled from the report's red-line warnings and from each hostile persona's forbidden_phrases reading>

**Must acknowledge:** <list of red-line crossings from the report that cannot be ignored — if the statement omits these, it will compound the problem>

**Tone directive:** <one sentence drawn from the report's consensus reading of the situation — e.g. "Direct, low-temperature, no defensive framing. Every hostile persona said defensive framing makes it worse.">
```

## Grounding rules

- Every claim in the statement traces to the factbase or the stimulus. No new facts.
- The statement must not cross a red_line surfaced in the report (check every persona's red_lines column).
- "Do not say" and "Must acknowledge" lists are drawn from the report — not improvised.
- The "Tone directive" cites the specific consensus signal(s) that support it.

## Length

~150-250 words total, including the do-not-say / must-acknowledge / tone directive sections.
